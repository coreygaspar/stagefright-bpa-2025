Stage Fright

Stage Fright is the band's self-titled first album, released on July 27th, 2022. As the album that started it all, Stage Fright introduced the world to an innovative sound that would come to dominate the Rock genre for years to come. The electrifying melodies of the album were Stage Fright's first foray into the music world.

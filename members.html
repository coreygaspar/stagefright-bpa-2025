Members

Corey Gaspar

Guitarist/Lead Vocalist

As the guitarist and lead vocalist of Stage Fright, Corey blends his guitar riffs with his vocals to create a unique sound for Stage Fright. Heavily influenced by other rock styles such as punk and indie rock, Corey strives to put passion and energy into every performance.

Felipe Zak

Bassist/Vocalist

As the bassist of Stage Fright, Felipe lays down the deep, driving grooves that give the band's sound its undeniable pulse. With a new style influenced by rock, funk, and a touch of experimental flair, Felipe brings a dynamic energy to every track.

Alexander Carrasco

Drummer

As the drummer of Stage Fright, Alex helps form the electrifying instrumentals on each one of the band's songs. Drawing inspiration from other genres such as punk, alternative rock, and beyond, Alex gives every track a loud and powerful intensity.

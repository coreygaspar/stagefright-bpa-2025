About Us

Born in the heart of Massachusetts, Stage Fright is an Indie Rock band dedicated to creating smooth sounds. Each song is infused with soul, passion, and most importantly, fright!

History

Stage Fright was formed in 2022 in Boston, MA, when Corey, Felipe, and Alex turned their jam sessions into a full-fledged band. Their sound blends various rock genres, from funk rock and indie to alternative and classic rock. Starting out in small bars, restaurants, and local music venues, the band quickly gained momentum and began performing across the country.

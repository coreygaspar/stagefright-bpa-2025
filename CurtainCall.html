Curtain Call

Curtain Call is the 3rd album by Stage Fright, released on December 31st, 2023. With this album, Stage Fright ends 2023 by combining the sounds of their previous two albums and merging them into a fresh and refined style. As a celebration and tribute to their success as a band so far, this album is Stage Fright's proverbial Curtain Call.
